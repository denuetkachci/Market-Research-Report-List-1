<p><p>The Horn Antennas market can be categorized into three main types: Twistable Waveguide, Seamless Non-Twistable, and Non-Twistable. Twistable Waveguide antennas allow for adjustable radiation patterns, making them versatile for various applications. Seamless Non-Twistable antennas are designed for efficient signal transmission without joints, minimizing losses. Non-Twistable antennas, on the other hand, provide fixed beam directions and are often used in applications requiring stable performance. Each type serves specific industry needs, from telecommunications to radar systems.</p></p>
