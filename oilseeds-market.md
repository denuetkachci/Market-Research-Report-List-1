<p><p>The oilseeds market encompasses various types of seeds cultivated primarily for oil extraction. Copra, derived from coconuts, is rich in saturated fat. Cottonseed, a byproduct of cotton production, yields oil with distinctive flavors. Palm kernel oil, extracted from the fruit of oil palms, is widely used in cooking. Peanut oil is favored for frying due to its high smoke point. Rapeseed, primarily used for canola oil, features a balanced fatty acid profile. Soybeans are versatile, offering both oil and protein, while sunflower seeds provide light, healthy oil.</p></p>
