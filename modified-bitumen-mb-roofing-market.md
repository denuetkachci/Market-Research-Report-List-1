<p><strong>The Modified Bitumen (MB) Roofing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SBS Type</li><li>APP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modified Bitumen (MB) roofing is categorized primarily into two types: SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) and APP (Atactic Polypropylene). SBS type is known for its flexibility and superior performance in varying temperatures, making it ideal for climates with extreme weather changes. APP type, on the other hand, offers enhanced resistance to ultraviolet rays and is more suitable for warmer climates. Both types are reinforced with a fiberglass or polyester mat, ensuring durability and effectiveness in waterproofing and protecting building structures.</p></p>
