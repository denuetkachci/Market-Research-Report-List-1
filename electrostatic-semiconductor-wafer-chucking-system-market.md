<p><strong>The Electrostatic Semiconductor Wafer Chucking System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrostatic semiconductor wafer chucking system market primarily features two types: Coulomb type electrostatic chucks and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type electrostatic chucks. Coulomb type chucks utilize electrical field forces to hold wafers securely during processing, ensuring minimal contamination and precise alignment. JR type electrostatic chucks, on the other hand, leverage both electrostatic attraction and vacuum forces to achieve superior wafer stability and thermal conductivity. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in semiconductor manufacturing, catering to diverse industry applications.</p></p>
