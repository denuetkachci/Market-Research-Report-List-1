<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Chromatography</li><li>Ion Exclusion Chromatography</li><li>Ion Pair Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ion chromatography systems market encompasses several key types. Ion Exchange Chromatography utilizes resin to separate ions based on their charge, ideal for analyzing cations and anions in various samples. Ion Exclusion Chromatography targets small organic acids and bases, allowing for the separation of uncharged species from ionic ones. Ion Pair Chromatography combines ionic and non-ionic interactions, facilitating the analysis of ions in complex mixtures, such as in pharmaceutical and environmental applications. Each type serves distinct analytical needs across industries.</p></p>
