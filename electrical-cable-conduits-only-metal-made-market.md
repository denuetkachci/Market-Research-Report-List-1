<p><p>The metal electrical cable conduits market is primarily divided into two types: flexible conduits and rigid conduits. Flexible conduits are designed to allow easy maneuverability and installation in tight spaces, providing protection while accommodating movement and vibration. Rigid conduits, on the other hand, offer a robust solution for straight runs, ensuring high structural integrity and excellent shielding against environmental factors. Both types serve essential roles in safeguarding electrical cables from physical damage and electromagnetic interference in various applications.</p></p>
