<p><ul><li>Atomic spectrometry</li><li>Molecular spectrometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical spectrometer market is divided into two main types: atomic spectrometry and molecular spectrometry. Atomic spectrometry focuses on the analysis of elemental composition through techniques like atomic absorption and emission spectroscopy, allowing for precise identification and quantification of metals and other elements. In contrast, molecular spectrometry involves the study of molecular structures and interactions, utilizing methods such as UV-Vis and infrared spectroscopy to analyze organic compounds and materials. Both types are essential for research, quality control, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
