<p><p>The automobile electronic power steering (EPS) system market is segmented into brushed and brushless types based on their motor design. Brushed EPS systems use traditional brushed motors, which are cheaper and simpler but may require more maintenance due to wear over time. Conversely, brushless EPS systems utilize advanced brushless motors that offer higher efficiency, longer lifespan, and quieter operation. As a result, brushless systems are increasingly preferred in modern vehicles for their performance benefits and reduced energy consumption.</p></p>
