Single-stage rotary vane pumps feature one set of vanes, offering simplicity and are ideal for low to medium vacuum applications. Two-stage rotary vane pumps employ two rotor stages to achieve deeper vacuum levels, suitable for more demanding industrial processes. Other types may include variations like multiple-stage or specialty pumps designed for specific applications, enhancing performance in unique environments or requirements.</p></p>
