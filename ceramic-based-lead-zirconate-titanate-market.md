<p><ul><li>Hard Lead Zirconate Titanate</li><li>Soft Lead Zirconate Titanate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic-based lead zirconate titanate (PZT) is categorized into hard and soft PZT types based on their properties and applications. Hard lead zirconate titanate exhibits high coercive fields and is characterized by its rigidity, making it suitable for high-frequency applications such as ultrasonic devices and actuators. In contrast, soft lead zirconate titanate has lower coercive fields and is more easily polarized, giving it enhanced dielectric properties, which are ideal for applications like capacitors and sensors.</p></p>
