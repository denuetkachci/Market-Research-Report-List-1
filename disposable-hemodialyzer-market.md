<p><ul><li>High-Flux Dialyzer</li><li>Low-Flux Dialyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable hemodialyzer market is divided into two main types: high-flux and low-flux dialyzers. High-flux dialyzers have larger pore sizes, allowing for the effective removal of larger molecules and providing better clearance rates for various toxins. They are often used for patients with more complex renal issues. In contrast, low-flux dialyzers have smaller pores and are primarily designed for the removal of small molecules, making them suitable for routine dialysis treatments where less intensive clearance is needed.</p></p>
