<p><p>Electric bike sharing services are categorized into two main market types: dockless and station-based. Dockless systems allow users to pick up and drop off bikes at any location, offering flexibility and convenience. Users locate bikes via a mobile app and park them anywhere within designated zones. In contrast, station-based systems require bikes to be rented and returned to specific docking stations. This model provides better tracking and management but may limit user spontaneity in bike access and drop-off.</p></p>
