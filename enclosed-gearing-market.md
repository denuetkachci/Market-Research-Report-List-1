<p><ul><li>Shaft Mounted Reducers</li><li>In-Line/Concentric Reducers</li><li>Off-Set/Parallel Reducers</li><li>Right Angle Reducers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enclosed gearing encompasses various reducer types essential for torque transmission in machinery. Shaft mounted reducers attach directly to the motor shaft, optimizing space and reducing alignment issues. In-line reducers maintain a straight alignment for efficient power transfer, while offset/parallel reducers allow for angled power delivery, commonly used in compact applications. Right angle reducers provide a 90-degree turn in power direction, ideal for tight spaces. Other market variants include custom solutions tailored for specific applications, enhancing flexibility in design.</p></p>
