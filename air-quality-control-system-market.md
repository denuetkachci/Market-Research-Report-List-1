<p><p>The Air Quality Control System market encompasses several technologies aimed at reducing emissions from industrial processes. Nitrogen Oxide Control Systems reduce NOx emissions, enhancing air quality. Fabric filters trap dust and particulates through a permeable fabric material. Mercury Control Systems are implemented to limit mercury emissions from combustion sources, protecting both health and environmental quality.</p></p>
