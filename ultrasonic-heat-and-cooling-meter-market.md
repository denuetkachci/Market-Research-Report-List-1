<p><p>The ultrasonic heat and cooling meter market consists of two main types: integrated and split meters. Integrated heat and cooling meters combine all components in one unit, offering simplified installation and maintenance, making them suitable for residential applications. In contrast, split heat and cooling meters have separate measuring and transmitting elements, allowing for greater flexibility in installation and easier access to measurement components. Both types utilize ultrasonic technology for precise flow measurement and energy calculation, enhancing energy management and efficiency.</p></p>
