<p><ul><li>Wire Mesh Defogger</li><li>Baffle Type Mist Eliminator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chrome Mist Eliminators market comprises two primary types: Wire Mesh Defoggers and Baffle Type Mist Eliminators. Wire Mesh Defoggers use a grid of fine wires to capture and coalesce liquid droplets from gas streams, enhancing efficiency in separation processes. In contrast, Baffle Type Mist Eliminators utilize a series of baffles that redirect gas flow, allowing droplets to coalesce and be removed. Both types are essential for maintaining air quality and efficiency in various industrial applications by preventing mist from escaping into the environment.</p></p>
