<p><ul><li>Vertically Transporting Substrates Sputtering Device</li><li>Horizontally Transporting Substrates Sputtering Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cell sputtering device market comprises two main types: vertically transporting substrates sputtering devices and horizontally transporting substrates sputtering devices. Vertically transporting devices allow substrates to move in a vertical direction, optimizing space and facilitating easier handling of multiple layers. Conversely, horizontally transporting devices enable substrates to move horizontally, often providing better uniformity and consistency during the sputtering process. Both types cater to various applications in electronics, photovoltaics, and thin-film technologies, influencing production efficiency and material quality.</p></p>
