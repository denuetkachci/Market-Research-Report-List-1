<p><ul><li>Wood Pulp</li><li>Primary Slurry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kitchen paper towel market can be categorized into two main types: wood pulp and primary slurry. Wood pulp towels are made from processed wood fibers, offering high absorbency and strength, making them popular for household use. Primary slurry towels, on the other hand, consist of a mixture of cellulose fibers and water, which are formed into sheets. While typically less absorbent than wood pulp, primary slurry products can be more eco-friendly, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers.</p></p>
