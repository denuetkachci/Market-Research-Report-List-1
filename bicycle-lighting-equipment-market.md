<p><ul><li>Halogen Lights</li><li>Xenon Lights</li><li>LED Lights</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bicycle lighting equipment market comprises three main types: halogen lights, xenon lights, and LED lights. Halogen lights offer bright illumination but consume more energy and have shorter lifespans. Xenon lights provide intense brightness and are energy-efficient, making them suitable for longer rides. However, they can be more expensive. LED lights dominate the market due to their low energy consumption, durability, and long lifespan, providing excellent visibility while being lightweight and cost-effective, appealing to a wide range of cyclists.</p></p>
