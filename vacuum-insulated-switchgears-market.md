<p><p>Vacuum-insulated switchgear (VIS) includes primary and secondary switchgear types, each serving distinct roles in electrical distribution. Primary switchgear is designed for high-voltage applications, handling significant power loads and ensuring reliable protection and control at transmission and distribution substations. Secondary switchgear, on the other hand, is utilized for lower voltage applications, typically found in industrial and commercial settings. Both types leverage vacuum technology to enhance insulation and interrupting performance, offering improved safety, reduced footprint, and maintenance advantages over traditional systems.</p></p>
