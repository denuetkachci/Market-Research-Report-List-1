<p><ul><li>In-line Depaneling Equipment</li><li>Off-line Depaneling Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The depaneling equipment market is divided into two main types: in-line and off-line depaneling equipment. In-line depaneling equipment is integrated into automated manufacturing processes, allowing continuous separation of PCBs from panels as they move through production. This enhances efficiency and reduces handling. Conversely, off-line depaneling equipment operates independently, enabling batch processing or manual handling, which is ideal for smaller production runs or specialized applications. Both types serve distinct operational needs in the electronics manufacturing sector.</p></p>
