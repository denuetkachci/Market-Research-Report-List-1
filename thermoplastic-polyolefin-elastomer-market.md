<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyolefin Elastomer (TPO) market includes two key types: Ethylene Propylene Monomer (EPM) and Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM). EPM is a rubbery material known for its excellent flexibility and low-temperature performance, primarily used in automotive and construction applications. EPDM, containing diene, enhances heat, ozone resistance, and weatherability, making it ideal for outdoor applications such as roofing and seals. Both types contribute to the growing demand for lightweight, durable materials across various industries, including automotive, construction, and consumer goods.</p></p>
