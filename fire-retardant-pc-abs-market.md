<p><p>The Fire Retardant PC-ABS market is primarily segmented into two types: Injection Molding and Extrusion Molding. Injection Molding involves shaping the material by injecting molten PC-ABS into molds, ideal for producing complex geometries and high-quality parts. Extrusion Molding, on the other hand, involves pushing the material through a die to create continuous shapes like sheets or profiles, used for larger components. Both methods leverage the flame-retardant properties of PC-ABS, catering to sectors requiring enhanced safety measures.</p></p>
