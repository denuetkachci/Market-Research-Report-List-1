<p><p>The three-point hitch cultivator market consists of three main types: hanging, semi-mounted, and mounted cultivators. Hanging cultivators are directly attached to the tractor's rear, providing maneuverability and stability for smaller fields. Semi-mounted cultivators combine features of both mounted and trailing designs, allowing for better weight distribution and adaptability on varied terrains. Finally, mounted cultivators are fully integrated into the tractor, offering enhanced control and efficiency. Each type caters to different agricultural needs and farming practices, enhancing soil preparation and crop management.</p></p>
