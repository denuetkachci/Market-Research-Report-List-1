<p><p>A turbine flow sensor is a type of flow meter that utilizes the mechanical rotation of a turbine rotor to measure the flow rate of liquids and gases. As fluid moves through the sensor, it causes the turbine blades to spin, with the rotational speed correlating directly to the flow rate.
