<p><p>Shuttle blister packaging systems are categorized into three market types based on their operational capabilities. Manual systems require operators to load products and initiate the packaging process, offering flexibility but lower output. Semi-automatic systems incorporate some mechanization, allowing operators to focus on loading while the machine handles sealing and forming, increasing efficiency. Automatic systems fully integrate automated processes, providing high-speed production with minimal human intervention, ideal for large-scale operations that demand consistency and volume in packaging.</p></p>
