<p><strong>The Portable Handheld VOC Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable handheld VOC monitor market is primarily categorized into two types: Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID monitors utilize ultraviolet light to ionize volatile organic compounds, providing real-time and sensitive detection of a wide range of VOCs. In contrast, MOS sensors detect VOCs through changes in electrical conductivity when gases interact with a metal-oxide layer. Each technology offers unique advantages in terms of sensitivity, response time, and operational environment, catering to diverse application needs.</p></p>
