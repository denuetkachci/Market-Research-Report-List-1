<p><ul><li>Time-Domain</li><li>Frequency-Domain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorescence Lifetime Imaging Microscopy (FLIM) is categorized into two primary market types: Time-Domain and Frequency-Domain. Time-Domain FLIM measures the time interval between excitation and emission of fluorescence, providing high temporal resolution for dynamic biological processes. In contrast, Frequency-Domain FLIM analyzes the modulation of fluorescence as a function of frequency, allowing for efficient data acquisition and noise reduction. Both methods are crucial for studying molecular interactions and cellular environments, enhancing our understanding of biological systems.</p></p>
