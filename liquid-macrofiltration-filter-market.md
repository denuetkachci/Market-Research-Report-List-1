<p><p>The Liquid Macrofiltration Filter market encompasses various types designed for diverse applications. Leaf Tubular and Press Filters combine surface filtration with tubular designs for efficient separation. Bag Filters trap particles in fabric bags, whereas Drum and Disk Filters rotate to maintain filtration. Belt Filter Presses dewater sludge through gravity and pressure, while AWB Filters employ advanced techniques for specific needs. Other filters cater to unique requirements, enhancing versatility in liquid processing.</p></p>
