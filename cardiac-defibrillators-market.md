<p><ul><li>Automatic External Defibrillators</li><li>Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators</li><li>Wearable Defibrillators</li><li>Advanced Life Support (ALS) Defibrillators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac defibrillators market includes various types tailored for different emergency situations. Automatic External Defibrillators (AEDs) are portable devices designed for layperson use in public settings. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) are surgically placed in patients at risk of life-threatening arrhythmias. Wearable Defibrillators are external, lightweight devices for patients awaiting ICD implantation. Advanced Life Support (ALS) Defibrillators are sophisticated devices used by medical professionals in hospitals and ambulances, equipped with monitoring and advanced therapeutic capabilities for complex cardiac emergencies.</p></p>
