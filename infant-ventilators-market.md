<p><p>The infant ventilators market comprises several types, including Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP), Noninvasive Positive Pressure Ventilation (NIPPV), and other specialized devices. CPAP delivers a constant airflow to keep airways open, aiding infants with respiratory distress. NIPPV provides breathing assistance through a mask, allowing infants to breathe spontaneously while reducing the work of breathing. Other market types may include invasive ventilators or hybrid systems, adapting to various clinical needs in neonatal care and enhancing support for premature or critically ill infants.</p></p>
