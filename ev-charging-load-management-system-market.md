<p><p>The EV Charging Load Management System market is categorized into dynamic and static management types. Dynamic management adjusts charging power in real-time based on factors like grid demand and available energy, optimizing usage and preventing overloads. In contrast, static management involves predetermined schedules and fixed charging rates, providing predictability but less adaptability to changing conditions. Both types aim to enhance grid stability and efficiency while catering to the growing demand for electric vehicle charging infrastructure.</p></p>
