<p><strong>The Bifacial Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>P-Type</li><li>N-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bifacial solar module market is primarily segmented into two types: P-Type and N-Type. P-Type modules use a boron-doped silicon substrate, typically offering lower efficiency and performance in shaded conditions. They are widely adopted due to their cost-effectiveness. In contrast, N-Type modules utilize phosphorus-doped silicon, allowing for higher efficiency, better temperature coefficients, and improved performance in low-light conditions. The N-Type market is growing due to its advancements in technology, making it increasingly attractive for utility-scale solar projects.</p></p>
