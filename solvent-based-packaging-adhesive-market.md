<p><p>The solvent-based packaging adhesive market is divided into two main types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are ready-to-use formulations that require no mixing, making them convenient for applications where speed and efficiency are crucial. In contrast, two-component adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener that must be mixed prior to application, offering enhanced strength and adhesion properties. Both types are utilized in various packaging applications, catering to different performance requirements and production processes.</p></p>
