<p><p>The surgery hemostat powder market consists of three main types: Microfibrillar Collagen, Chitosan, and Other variants. Microfibrillar Collagen is derived from collagen and promotes hemostasis by providing a scaffold for platelets and promoting clot formation. Chitosan, sourced from chitin, acts as a natural anticoagulant and aids in the rapid control of bleeding.
