<p><p>The enema-based products market comprises several types, each serving distinct purposes. Cleansing enemas are primarily used for bowel preparation and detoxification. Carminative enemas relieve gas and bloating by easing intestinal discomfort. Retention enemas are designed to be held in the rectum for therapeutic effects, often employing medications or nutrients. Return-flow enemas promote the evacuation of gas or stools through a repeated process of instillation and expulsion, aiding in bowel regulation and relief from discomfort.</p></p>
