<p><strong>The E Glass Fiber Yarn Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fiber Yarn</li><li>Fiber Roving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The E-Glass fiber yarn market primarily consists of two types: fiber yarn and fiber roving. Fiber yarn is produced by spinning continuous strands of E-Glass fibers, which are used in various composites and textiles due to their lightweight and strength. Fiber roving, on the other hand, comprises bundles of glass fibers that are loosely twisted together, making them ideal for applications requiring bulk and flexibility, such as reinforcement in composite materials. Both products are essential in industries like construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
