<p><p>The hydrogen ejector market is primarily segmented into single-stage and dual-stage ejectors. Single-stage ejectors utilize one jet to convert high-pressure hydrogen energy into low-pressure flow, making them suitable for simpler applications. In contrast, dual-stage ejectors employ two jets to enhance efficiency and performance, allowing for greater pressure reduction and improved flow management. Both types serve industrial purposes in hydrogen production, transport, and storage, emphasizing energy efficiency and system optimization to support the growing hydrogen economy.</p></p>
