Gas lasers, such as CO2 lasers, offer high precision and are used in cutting and engraving. Liquid lasers, like dye lasers, provide tunability for applications in spectroscopy. Semiconductor lasers are compact and energy-efficient, widely used in telecommunications and consumer electronics. Each type plays a crucial role across diverse sectors.</p></p>
