<p><p>The Frequency Response Analyzer market is categorized by waveform types: sine wave, square wave, and triangle wave. Sine wave analyzers are used for smooth signal tests, offering precise frequency response measurements ideal for audio equipment. Square wave analyzers help test digital devices, as they can quickly switch states, revealing system stability and bandwidth. Triangle wave analyzers, with their linear rise and fall, are beneficial for testing filter circuits and confirming system behavior over a range of frequencies. Each waveform serves unique applications in electronics testing and design.</p></p>
