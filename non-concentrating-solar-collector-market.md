<p><p>Non-concentrating solar collectors are devices that capture solar energy without using mirrors or lenses to focus sunlight. These collectors typically include flat-plate and evacuated tube collectors, which absorb solar radiation directly to heat water or air for various applications, including residential water heating, space heating, and industrial processes. The simplicity, durability, and cost-effectiveness of non-concentrating solar collectors make them popular choices for both small and large-scale solar projects.</p><p>The market for non-concentrating solar collectors is poised for significant growth, driven by increasing awareness of renewable energy, supportive government policies, and technological advancements.
